### Below the ‚â•95 bar ‚Äî explained

- **`/atlas` perf 81.** Sprint 3 known issue. The atlas section gallery mixes local images with raw `https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/...` URLs that Sharp can't optimize. Mitigation: add an explicit `heroImage: getSqImage('atlas_hero.webp')` (already done ‚Äî that's why hero LCP is fast); the residual hit is from Squarespace-CDN images in the `product` section. Fix at content time by uploading those images locally. Tracked in `SPRINT-3-QA.md`.
- **`/thoughts/knowledge-graph` best-practices 77.** The Figma iframe at `figma.com/embed?...` sets third-party cookies (`ajs_anonymous_id`, `AWSALBTGCORS`, `AWSALBCORS`). Browser-side and unavoidable without a click-to-load wrapper. The post is essential to the page. Same root cause for `/thoughts/blind-data` (Spotify) and `/thoughts/payment-processing` (Spotify) at 77.
- **`/thoughts/fundraising-course/step-0` best-practices 96.** YouTube iframe similarly sets cookies but with smaller penalty. Within tolerance.

### Click-to-load wrapper ‚Äî recommended for v1.1

If launch needs every page ‚â•95, wrap Figma/Spotify embeds in a button that swaps in the iframe on click. Defers cookie-setting until the user opts in. ~30 LOC component change. Out of scope for v1.
