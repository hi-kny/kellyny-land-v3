A guest lecture series for undergraduates exploring what it means to actually *start something* — not the deck-pitching highlight reel, but the unglamorous middle.

The class spans the founder mindset (failure as input, not output), early-stage business basics (PMF, GTM, fundraising 101), and the small set of skills that actually compound: writing, sales, and pattern recognition.
