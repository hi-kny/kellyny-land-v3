**Podcast Mini-Series — Created & Produced by Kelly Nyland, Rhenee Bartlett & Ryan Hornberger.**

It all begins with an idea. Maybe you want to launch a business. Maybe you want to turn a hobby into something more. Or maybe you have a creative project to share with the world.

Payment processing sits at the bottom of every commerce stack — boring, foundational, and completely opaque to most operators. We unpack the layers, explain who's making money where, and walk through what changes when stablecoins, instant settlement, and AI-driven fraud detection collide with an industry that's been running on the same rails for two decades.
